How We Started

Founded in 2020, our store was born out of a passion for cutting-edge mobile technology. As longtime Google Pixel users ourselves, we realized that finding the latest models, accessories, and the best deals in one place was a challenge. So, we set out to create a one-stop shop dedicated solely to Google Pixel devices.
